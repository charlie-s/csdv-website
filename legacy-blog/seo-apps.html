SEO Apps

Performing SEO can be a time consuming task. While proper (non-spam) SEO should be done by hand, there are a few apps and other quick methods out there that are useful for keyword analysis & generally making you more efficient at the process.

http://www.advancedwebranking.com/ - Advanced Web Ranking
This Windows/Mac/Linux app provides a very detailed look into the keywords, rankings, popularity, and other analytical aspects of sites. One of the big benefits of this app is the report generation that can show your clients more broad trends over a given amount of time. Google Analytics is great for Google's results, but this will give you a bigger picture of what's out there.
